## Pitch Deck Outline

1. Title: Nexta - The pathway platform for opportunity and economic progress
2. Problem: opportunity is fragmented, people drop off after discovery, and organisations struggle to measure outcomes beyond views and applications
3. Insight: progress is a pathway, not a moment
4. Solution: discovery -> readiness -> application -> support -> retention -> progression -> independence
5. Product overview:
   - Jobs and career pathways
   - Learning and credentials
   - Mentors and sessions
   - Community and connection
   - AI guidance and opportunity radar
   - Business tools and templates
   - Financial wellbeing tools
   - Housing and stability features
   - Employer and institutional dashboards
   - Public sector and procurement workflows
6. Why now: skills transitions, AI disruption, cost-of-living pressure, and demand for measurable outcomes
7. Who it's for:
   - Individuals
   - Mentors and facilitators
   - Employers and recruiters
   - Education and training providers
   - Government and enterprise partners
8. Differentiation:
   - Pathway-based
   - Whole-of-life support
   - Ecosystem workflows
   - Safety, trust, and user agency
   - AI for guidance and next steps
9. Business model:
   - B2C freemium + premium tools
   - B2B employer seats and analytics
   - B2G/B2E program deployments and reporting
   - Marketplace services and offers
10. Go-to-market:
    - Start with jobs + learning + guidance
    - Expand through employers, providers, and partners
    - Deepen retention with pathways, mentors, and community
    - Scale with dashboards and reporting
11. Traction:
    - Product modules shipped
    - Active cohorts and pilot partners
    - Engagement, completion, and retention
    - Case studies and outcomes
12. Roadmap:
    - Deeper personalised pathways
    - Stronger radar
    - Marketplace and procurement expansion
    - Mobile, messaging, and notifications
    - Safety and trust tooling
    - Stronger analytics and outcomes
13. Team:
    - Founder story
    - Engineering and product capability
    - Advisors and partners
14. Ask:
    - Funding range
    - What it unlocks
    - Phase-based milestones
    - Contact

## Brand Voice

Brand essence:
Nexta is the calm, capable guide for real progress.
Not hype. Not judgement. Just clarity, tools, and momentum.

Voice pillars:
- Clear
- Supportive
- Respectful
- Practical
- Optimistic

Tone by context:
- Onboarding: welcoming, simple, non-overwhelming
- Guidance and AI: coach-like, structured, actionable
- Notifications: short, useful, low-noise
- Errors: calm, helpful, no blame
- Enterprise and government: confident, outcomes-driven, measurable

Words we use:
- Pathway
- Next step
- Progress
- Tools
- Guidance
- Stability
- Outcomes
- Verified
- You're in control

Words we avoid:
- Hack
- Crush it
- Dominate
- Hustle
- Life-changing overnight
- Shame-based language

Formatting rules:
- Short sentences
- Headings and scannable sections
- Action verbs for CTAs
- Always offer the next step at the end
