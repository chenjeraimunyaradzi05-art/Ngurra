# Privacy Policy - Ngurra Pathways (Gimbi)

*Last Updated: [Current Date]*

## 1. Introduction

Ngurra Pathways ("we", "us", "our") operates the Gimbi platform (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy complies with the Australian Privacy Act 1988, the Privacy (Credit Reporting) Code 2014, and GDPR requirements where applicable.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, password (hashed)
- **Profile Information**: Professional background, skills, education, work experience
- **Identity Verification**: Government-issued ID (for verified accounts)
- **Application Data**: Job applications, cover letters, resumes
- **Communication Data**: Messages, feedback, support requests
- **Payment Information**: Payment card details (processed via Stripe; we don't store full card numbers)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session cookies, analytics cookies (see Cookie Policy)
- **Log Data**: Server logs, error reports, performance metrics

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Social Login**: Profile data from Google/LinkedIn if you choose social sign-in
- **Employer Data**: Company information provided by employers on the platform
- **Background Checks**: With your consent, verification results from third-party providers

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide Services**: Match candidates with job opportunities, facilitate applications
- **Improve Platform**: Analyze usage patterns, develop new features
- **Communicate**: Send notifications, updates, marketing (with consent)
- **Security**: Detect fraud, protect against abuse, maintain platform integrity
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements, respond to legal requests
- **Support**: Provide customer service and technical support

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal bases include:

- **Contract Performance**: Processing necessary to provide our services
- **Legitimate Interests**: Platform security, fraud prevention, service improvement
- **Consent**: Marketing communications, non-essential cookies
- **Legal Obligation**: Tax records, regulatory compliance

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 With Your Consent

- Sharing profile data with potential employers when you apply
- Sharing with third-party services you choose to connect

### 5.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted providers who assist our operations:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| AWS | Cloud hosting | All platform data (encrypted) |
| Stripe | Payment processing | Payment details |
| SendGrid | Email delivery | Email addresses, content |
| Cloudinary | Image storage | Profile photos |

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information:

- To comply with legal obligations
- To respond to lawful requests from government authorities
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

### 5.4 We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Active account data | Duration of account + 2 years |
| Inactive accounts | 2 years after last activity |
| Job applications | 3 years (per recruitment records requirements) |
| Payment records | 7 years (tax compliance) |
| Security logs | 1 year |
| Deleted account data | 30 days (soft delete), then permanent |

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access and Portability

You can:
- Access your data via Settings > Privacy > Download My Data
- Request a machine-readable export of your data

### 7.2 Correction

You can update incorrect data through your profile settings or by contacting support.

### 7.3 Deletion

You can:
- Delete specific data (e.g., resume, applications)
- Request full account deletion via Settings > Privacy > Delete Account
- Request deletion by emailing privacy@ngurrapathways.com.au

### 7.4 Restriction and Objection

You can:
- Restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Opt out of marketing communications at any time

### 7.5 Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw at any time through your account settings.

## 8. Data Security

We implement robust security measures:

- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, principle of least privilege
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication, secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring, intrusion detection
- **Auditing**: Regular security audits, penetration testing
- **Incident Response**: Documented procedures, breach notification within 72 hours

## 9. International Data Transfers

For users outside Australia, your data may be transferred to our servers in Australia. We ensure appropriate safeguards:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EEA transfers
- Binding Corporate Rules where applicable
- Privacy Shield certification (where relevant)

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.
