Build Long-Term Careers with Cultural Guidance

Ngurra Pathways (Gimbi) is a First Nations-designed digital ecosystem uniting job discovery, culturally safe mentorship, education partnerships, and community storytelling. Connect with trusted employers, get matched with mentors who share your background, and grow with guidance that respects Country, culture, and wellbeing.
